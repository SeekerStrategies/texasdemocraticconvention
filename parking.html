Parking

Convention Center parking is located at 650 S. Griffin Street in Dallas, TX 75202. The parking garage and three surface parking lots (Lots C, D, and E) can accommodate almost any vehicle. For a more detailed FAQ on parking at the convention center, please click here. Please note: the parking garage hours are 5am-12am midnight. Overnight parking is not allowed.

Texas Democratic Convention attendees may purchase parking in advance here: txdem.co/Convention-Parking
